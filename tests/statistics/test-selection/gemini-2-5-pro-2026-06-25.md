### **Approach 1: PERMANOVA (for Beta Diversity)**

1.  **Test/method name**: **Permutational Multivariate Analysis of Variance** (PERMANOVA). It is often implemented via the `adonis2` function in the `vegan` R package.
2.  **When appropriate**: When you want to test if the centroids (multivariate locations) of two or more groups are different in a high-dimensional space, based on a distance matrix. 3.  **Key assumptions**:

    *   **Homogeneity of multivariate dispersion**: This is the most critical assumption. It means the within-group spread (variance) of the data points is similar between groups.
