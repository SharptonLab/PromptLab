**1. Transparency of Multiple Testing Correction:**

We have now added this information to the Methods section.

> **Revised Text (Page X, Lines Y-Z):** "Spearman’s rank correlation coefficients were calculated to assess associations between the relative abundances of 77 bacterial taxa and the concentrations of 11 key metabolites, resulting in a total of 847 comparisons. Associations were considered statistically significant at an FDR-adjusted p-value (q-value) of < 0.05."

**2. Interpretation of Weak Correlations:**

> **Newly Added Text:** "We also acknowledge that some of the statistically significant correlations identified in our analysis have modest effect sizes (|r| < 0.3). Future mechanistic studies will be essential to validate these more subtle relationships and determine their functional impact in the context of IBD."
